Love of Books

The habit of reading should be brought to individuals from a very young age. Even if children do not know how to read and write, it is necessary to experience the process of getting acquainted with picture books. Children who gain a love of books will continue this throughout their lives. Developing, thinking creatively, learning moral values ​​is possible with the habit of reading books. Books have a great contribution to the personal development and intelligence development of individuals.
 It is recommended that individuals read books with quality content. The topics covered in the book also help people gain moral maturity. Appropriateness for age is known as an important criterion in the selection of books. Books for all ages should be different. The suitability of the written language and the suitability of the subject gain importance.

 What are the Ways to Make Children Love Reading?

 Children who grow up in an environment with books are more inclined towards books. In order to instill a love of books, it is important to present the book to children as a development tool, not a task. In the process of gaining a habit of books, parents' reading books at home accelerates the process of love for the child.

 It is necessary to start with colorful books that contain quality content as a gift. Children should see that reading is a fun and instructive activity. Children will not enjoy the book they are reading when it is not appropriate for their age. This may cause children to move away from the book.

How Should Students Choose a Book Appropriate for Their Ages?

 The books that students read should be of the type that will support their creativity. The love of books is in direct proportion to the information learned from the book. If people see the effect of what they learn in life, their adherence to books will increase. Books aimed at gaining a habit or changing some habits can be chosen.

 Books with quality content should be books that develop children's imagination, increase their self-confidence, and enable them to have foresight. Preferring picture books at young ages is more positive in terms of age appropriateness. After the complete literacy, stories and novels on animal love, social awareness, science can be read.

 What Kind of Books Should Be Chosen at What Age?

 Colorful animated books should be bought for children aged 1-2. The love of books can be gained from this age. Between the ages of 2-5, the child can now receive some messages. The habit-forming process gets stronger. It is necessary to choose books that will improve the vocabulary.

 Quality content is important for the age group of 5-6. It is the period when children's imagination is the richest. Age appropriateness is a criterion that should be considered in the selection of books. After the age of 6, reading is learned. Books containing moral values, heroism and scientific subjects are suitable.

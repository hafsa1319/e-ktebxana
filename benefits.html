Child Benefits

“A child without books is like a tree that grows without water,” says the Chinese proverb. Based on this, we want to turn our children's hearts into a lush forest. We want to look at them from different windows and help them see the world in color. We offer all opportunities to our children to make them love reading and to have fun reading. We offer a selection of books suitable for all ages and all levels. You can also access the tests here to measure how well they understand what they read. They will develop their ability to know themselves correctly and to express their thoughts correctly by reading. Children who read:

- They can express themselves correctly.

- They fully express their feelings and thoughts.

- Intelligence levels will be higher.

- They have a strong memory.

- Develops written and verbal expressions.

- It does not look at events and situations from a single window, but from a wide window.

Parents Benefits

Children's first school is their family. Their first teachers are their parents. Learns the first behaviors about life in the family. Meanwhile, the family has great responsibilities. Parents should treat their children the way they want their children to be and set an example for them. The habit of reading books starts in the family and continues with the teachers. The family has a great responsibility here. If the family does not set an example in reading, the child cannot gain this behavior. Reading hours can be made at a time when everyone is at home. Even small competitions and competitions, small prizes can make reading more attractive. Remember, the behaviors that you do not do but that you want the child to do will not be seen in the child. Parents must set an example. Reading parents:

- It establishes the right bond with the child.

- They help them in their children's education.

- They are at peace with life and with themselves.

- They treat their children like friends.

- They are aware of technological developments.

- They do not experience much of the generation gap with their children.

Teacher Benefits

The education system is now based on the "lifelong education" model. This shows that learning and reading is a lifelong process. A lot of work falls on teachers here to make reading a habit. Teachers need to be good role models for their students. For this, they must first love to read so that students will love to read. Classroom teachers should conduct reading activities with children in order to set an example. They can even make reading more fun by doing small competitions. Small prizes, group or individual competitions will make students more enthusiastic. In this way, each student will learn how much they understand and think about the book. It reinforces literacy for students who have just learned to read. Thus, it avoids oblivion. Teachers studying:

- Express yourself more accurately.

- It avoids misunderstandings.

- It develops itself and keeps up with the times more easily.

- It closely follows current events and new developments.

- The teacher-student relationship becomes stronger.

- Can see life through the eyes of the student.

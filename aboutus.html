About us

As My-lesson, we know very well the importance of reading in human life, and for this reason, we open the door to a different world for you. If you would like to go on this mysterious and adventurous journey with us, we welcome you to our family. Reading develops one's imagination. It offers the individual the opportunity to look at life from different perspectives. It gives the ability to empathize. It provides a more accurate understanding of others. The habit of reading should be brought in the child at early ages. For this reason, a love of books should be infused to children by purchasing small picture books. The book level is changed as they grow. Here you can find the best quality books for all levels and all ages. We end going to the library for a book and searching for resources. With our wide range of products, we offer hundreds of resources in every field to our precious readers. You have the opportunity to read at home, at school or in the park whenever you want. We offer not only reading books, but also the opportunity to improve yourself with helpful resources in school lessons and follow the topics from different sources. If you want, you can subject to the exam and assess how much you understand and think at the end of the book. We have tried to make reading the most fun for you. It is on you to take part in this pleasant journey.

We invite everyone who loves to read to this magical world. We are happy to see parents, children, teachers and all book lovers who say that there is no age to read and learn. The world will get better as you read.

Reading guides the person in many different areas and gives a perspective that separates the person from the others.
       Of course, reading isn't just for kids. Reading is also very important for adults. Many problems that arise in advancing ages are minimized by the habit of constant reading. Reading saves people from the feeling of loneliness and worthlessness.

Reading is to have a new world in itself.


Reading is to have a new world in itself. You take the characters you want into this world that you have built yourself, and you give them roles in the way you want. No one can interfere in this area that belongs to you, no one can enter there without your permission.

Every age has its own world. While creating this area, the people should choose books according to his/her own level. The dream world of a 6-year-old child and the imagination of an 18-year-old teenager are not the same and are very different. What matters is who will read what. Every book is not suitable for every age level or lifestyle. The main task in the selection of books falls first on the family and then on the teachers. The family is the most important building block that makes their children love to read.

It is the best to choose books suitable for children's age and to read the books themselves first. Children read the book when they see appropriate. Likewise, teachers at school should recommend books according to the level of their students. The level of each age group is different. The right choices always make people's life easier. Choosing the right book also ensures that the person perceives correctly and prepares for life correctly.
